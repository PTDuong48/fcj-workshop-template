During my 12-week internship at **First Cloud Journey (FCJ)** from **05/04/2026** to **10/07/2026**, I had the opportunity to learn, practice, and apply Cloud Computing knowledge in a real-world working environment.

I went through two main phases: a **self-study and hands-on AWS practice** phase (Weeks 1–7) covering over 60 Labs; and a **team project** phase (Weeks 8–12) designing and developing the final project **DocuFlow AI** — an intelligent invoice processing system leveraging Amazon Textract, Step Functions, Bedrock, DynamoDB, and various Serverless services on AWS.

In terms of work ethic, I always strived to complete assigned tasks on schedule, proactively asked questions, and collaborated with teammates to find the best technical solutions.

### Needs Improvement

* **Time Management:** Need to better manage time, especially during intensive team-project phases, to ensure a consistent and steady pace of progress.
* **Problem-Solving Mindset:** Need to further develop the ability to debug and analyze system errors quickly and systematically, particularly in distributed environments (Serverless / Event-driven).
* **Technical Communication:** Improve the ability to clearly and concisely present and explain architectural decisions when reporting to Mentors and teammates.
